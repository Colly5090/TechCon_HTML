Our History

TechCon started in 2010 with a mission to bring together the brightest minds in technology, engineering, and innovation. Over the past decade, it has grown into one of the largest and most influential tech conferences globally. Each year, TechCon attracts thousands of attendees from around the world, fostering collaboration, networking, and groundbreaking technological advancements.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is simple yet powerful: to inspire, educate, and connect individuals who are shaping the future of technology. We aim to bridge the gap between emerging trends and real-world applications, empowering professionals to solve the world’s most pressing problems through innovation and creativity. TechCon is a space where tech enthusiasts and industry leaders gather to share knowledge, explore new ideas, and drive progress.

Notable Past Speakers


Elon Musk – Visionary entrepreneur and CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, who shared his insights on space exploration and the future of electric vehicles.


Sundar Pichai – CEO of Google and Alphabet, who discussed the role of artificial intelligence in everyday life.


Tim Berners-Lee – Inventor of the World Wide Web, who reflected on the past and future of the internet.
